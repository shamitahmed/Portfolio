About

Hello! I am Shamit, I am a professional Game Developer currently studying for my master's degree in Computer, Communication and Information Sciences(CCIS), majoring in Game Design and Development at Aalto University, Finland. Engaging video games, immersive-interactive graphics and breathtaking art of various forms have always been my motivation to achieve more. I have an immense interest in serious games and research related to education and healthcare. I envision myself as either a researcher working in these fields or an industry professional.
